3.Thupakki


Director : A.R.Murugadas

Writers : Mayank Jain & A.R. Murugadoss

Stars : Joseph Vijay, Kajal Aggarwal & Vidyut Jammwal


Thuppakki was released on 13 November 2012, Diwali day, and distributed by Gemini Film Circuit. The film went through numerous controversies, including protests from Islamic fringe groups who objected to the portrayal of the Islamic community in the film. Nevertheless, it received mostly positive reviews and became a major commercial success, grossing over ₹100 crore (equivalent to ₹189 crore or US$24 million in 2023) within 10 days of its release.
